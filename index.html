Privacy Policy

Deskdays
Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Privacy First: Deskdays is built with your privacy as a priority. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

Introduction

Welcome to Deskdays, a macOS menubar calendar app with customizable themes and countdowns. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

The bottom line: We don't collect any personal data. Your calendar images, countdowns, and settings are stored locally on your Mac and never leave your device.

Information We Don't Collect

Deskdays does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Device identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Browsing history or web data
  • Crash reports sent to external servers

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your Mac and never transmitted anywhere:

Countdowns

  • Countdown titles and dates you create
  • Custom images you choose for countdown backgrounds
  • Gradient and theme preferences

Calendar Customization

  • Month-specific images you upload
  • Theme selections (seasons, minimalist, etc.)
  • Hemisphere preference (Northern/Southern)

App Settings

  • Week start preference (Monday/Sunday/Saturday)
  • Launch at login preference

Local Error Logs

  • Crash logs are stored locally on your device for debugging purposes
  • These logs are never automatically transmitted to us
  • You have full control and can delete them anytime

All this data is stored in secure local storage on your Mac and is protected by macOS sandboxing.

In-App Purchases

All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment information (credit cards, billing details, Apple ID, etc.). Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase-related data.

For details, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

None. Deskdays does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is built entirely using Apple's native frameworks with zero third-party dependencies.

Network Activity

Deskdays makes no network requests except for communication with Apple's StoreKit service to verify your premium purchase status when you make a purchase or restore purchases.

No data is sent to any server we control or operate.

Photos and Images

When you choose custom images for your calendar or countdowns:

  • Images are selected from your Mac's photo library or file system
  • Images are stored securely within the app's protected storage on your Mac
  • Images never leave your device
  • You can delete images at any time through the app

We do not access your photo library beyond the images you explicitly choose to use.

Children's Privacy

Deskdays does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, the app is safe for all ages.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists only as long as you keep the app installed
  • Uninstalling Deskdays removes all app data including countdowns, images, and settings
  • Trial and premium status information is managed through standard macOS security mechanisms

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app or this page
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request, export, or delete from our side. You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is on your Mac in the app
  • Export: Images and settings are in your local file system
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
