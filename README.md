## About Deskdays

Deskdays is a native macOS menubar app that provides:

- **Calendar View** - Quick access to monthly calendar from your menubar
- **Custom Themes** - Choose from preset themes or upload your own photos for each month
- **Countdowns** - Track important dates with beautiful, auto-rotating countdowns
- **Minimal Design** - Clean, distraction-free interface that stays out of your way
